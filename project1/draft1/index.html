Akihabara District, Japan

Introduction

Akihabara is a famous town in Tokyo, Japan. It is a center that sells many varieties of electronics and Japanese and anime goods.

Many shops and establishments devoted to anime and manga are dispersed amongst the electronic stores in the district.
